# Future community product catalogue

This feature is not part of the initial release.

## Goal

Allow users to contribute and retrieve non-personal product information to reduce repeated scanning and manual entry.

## Allowed data

- Barcode/GTIN
- Product and brand name
- Package quantity
- Serving and portion units
- Nutrition values
- Ingredients, allergens, and additives
- Data-source provenance
- Verification date

## Prohibited data

- Contributor identity of any kind
- Stable pseudonymous identifiers
- Device or account identifiers
- Meal history or consumption amounts
- Dates or times of consumption
- Goals or body data
- Favourites, usage frequency, or private notes

## Approach

Evaluate contribution to Open Food Facts or compatibility with its format before operating an independent central database. App-specific data may later focus on practical portions such as verified slice or piece weights.
