# Privacy statement

Nutri Raccoon is being designed as a local-first nutrition tracker.

## Planned defaults

- No mandatory account.
- No advertising.
- No proprietary analytics or tracking SDKs.
- Personal meal history and nutrition goals stored locally.
- Network access used only for clearly identified optional functions such as product lookup, updates, or user-enabled AI assistance.
- Camera images processed locally where practical and never uploaded without explicit user action and disclosure.

## Community product catalogue

A future contribution feature may share sanitised product information such as barcode, name, package quantity, nutrition values, ingredients, allergens, and verification date.

It must never share contributor identity, meal history, consumption dates, goals, body data, favourites, usage frequency, private notes, device identifiers, or image metadata.

A complete, release-specific privacy policy will be published before testing with real user data.
