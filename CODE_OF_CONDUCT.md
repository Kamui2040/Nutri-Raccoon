# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, discuss technical and design disagreements constructively, and avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or disclosure of another person's private information.

## Project-specific privacy expectation

Do not request or publish personal nutrition records, medical details, private photographs, account information, or identifying diagnostic data. Reports should use synthetic examples and sanitised logs.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when necessary to protect contributors, users, or the project.

This concise policy may be replaced by a recognised community code of conduct before wider public participation begins.
